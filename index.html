About Me

Hello I am George a gamer turned programmer.

My love for games started back in the Crash and Spyro days playing on my dad's Playstation 1. In 2016 I decided to devote my passion for playing games and to start making them, I joined SAE Technology Group Athens and got my Degree from there. I have made numerous games and also helped as a Level Designer in the Steam game "Mark One". My love for programming and designing games is never ending but I also love making Tools in Unity to enchance the UX of the engine.
